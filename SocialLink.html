The Lovers Arcana

Bound by fate, entwined in passion. Two souls stand at the edge of eternity, their choices shaping the paths of both heart and spirit. The Lovers Arcana is not merely about love, but the harmony—or chaos—that emerges when two wills intertwine.

The card speaks of union, trust, and the trials that test their bond. For in the shadow of love lies its mirror—sacrifice, loss, and longing.

Luis Garcia
